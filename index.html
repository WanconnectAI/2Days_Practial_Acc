What You Will Learn 您将学到什么

Nine Modules, One Complete Set of Accounts九大模块，成就一套完整账目

Every module builds on the last — by Module 7, you're generating a full set of accounts yourself. 每个模块环环相扣——到第七模块，您已能亲手产出完整账目。

MODULE 01

The Entrepreneur's Financial Lens

Understand why accounting is a growth lever, not a compliance chore, and master the 5 stages of the accounting system.

了解会计如何成为企业成长的推动力，并掌握会计系统的五个阶段。
MODULE 02

Decoding Financial Statements

Read your own financial statements with confidence, including accruals, prepayments and the full accounting cycle.

自信解读财务报表，包括应计、预付款项与完整会计周期。
MODULE 03

The Revenue Cycle

Correctly account for sales, returns, discounts and customer payments, and manage Accounts Receivable control accounts.

正确处理销售、退货、折扣与客户付款，并管理应收账款控制账户。
MODULE 04

Procurement-to-Pay

Account for purchases, returns and supplier payments, and confidently distinguish capital from revenue expenditure.

妥善处理采购、退货与供应商付款，清楚区分资本性与收益性支出。
MODULE 05

Cash Management & Asset Protection

Run a bank reconciliation hands-on, manage modern payment methods, and set provisions for bad and doubtful debts.

亲手完成银行对账，掌握现代支付方式，设定坏账与呆账准备金。
MODULE 06

Year-End Adjustments & Errors

Track depreciation, manage payroll entries, and confidently identify and correct accounting errors.

追踪折旧、处理薪资分录，发现并更正会计错误。
MODULE 07

Full-Set Accounts & Reporting

Generate a complete set of accounts — P&L, Balance Sheet and Cashflow — through a hands-on mock exam exercise.

透过实战模拟练习，产出完整的损益表、资产负债表与现金流量表。
MODULE 08

AI Overview for Accountants

A big-picture look at generative AI vs. traditional automation, and where tools for drafting, parsing and compliance genuinely fit — an overview, not a hands-on certification.

概览生成式AI与传统自动化的分别，以及相关工具在草拟、解析与合规上的实际应用——属概览性质，非实操认证。
MODULE 09

Compliance, Audit & Beyond

Handle your auditor and tax agent more effectively, and leave with solutions to the practical problems that come up most often.

更有效应对审计师与税务代理，带走最常见实务问题的解决方案。
